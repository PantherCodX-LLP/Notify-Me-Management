# Marketing Plan — Notify Me / Back in Stock (Shopify App)

**Goal:** more installs + more revenue. **Listing:** https://apps.shopify.com/notifyme
**How to read this:** four channels, each with concrete steps and how-to. The dashboard's **Marketing** tab turns these into a trackable checklist. Sources are summarized at the end; full benchmarks live in `docs/GROWTH_STRATEGY.md`.

> **Reality check from the live listing:** the app is Built for Shopify with a strong rating but ~100 reviews, while top competitors (STOQ, Notify!, Essent) have 2,800–3,000. Reviews drive both ranking and conversion, so **closing the review gap is the single highest-impact move.**

---

## Priority quick-start (first 30 days)

1. Add a **neutral, opt-in in-app review prompt** (App Bridge `shopify.reviews.request()`) after a value moment, and run the **Review-request email campaign** (Email Outreach tab) to opted-in merchants.
2. **Reply to every existing review**; resolve recurring 1-star themes, then ask those merchants to reconsider (no incentive).
3. Put **primary keywords in the app name/tagline** and fill **all 5 hidden search terms** in the Partner Dashboard.
4. Buy **App Store search + category ads** on your category keywords.
5. Publish **2–3 comparison/"alternative" SEO pages** and pitch **"best back-in-stock apps" roundups**.

---

## Channel 1 — App Store Optimization (free, highest ROI)

Over 70% of installs come from App Store search. Reviews, keyword relevance, install velocity, low uninstall rate, and Built for Shopify status drive ranking.

- **Reviews (the #1 lever).** Confirmed Shopify ranking + conversion signal; the rating is weighted toward recent, trustworthy reviews. Ask **neutrally** ("We value feedback! Let us know how we're doing"), **never** incentivize, **never** ask only for positive reviews, **never** email without opt-in, **never** ask during onboarding. Deep link to the review modal: `https://apps.shopify.com/notifyme#modal-show=WriteReviewModal`.
- **Reply to all reviews** from Partner Dashboard → Apps. Helpful replies to negatives often prompt a higher rating and reassure prospective installers.
- **Keywords in name & tagline.** Name ≤ 30 chars (brand + keyword), tagline ≤ 62. Target "back in stock", "restock alert", "notify me", "preorder", "waitlist".
- **Fill all 5 hidden search terms** with high-intent 2–4 word phrases.
- **Description & screenshots.** 100–2,800 chars, plain, benefit-led. Avoid keyword-stuffing, superlatives ("best/first/only"), and outcome guarantees (editorial rejection). Add keyword-rich ALT text to every screenshot; lead with a benefit-focused first screenshot + demo video.
- **Maintain Built for Shopify.** Keep admin Web Vitals (LCP ≤2.5s, CLS ≤0.1, INP ≤200ms), storefront Lighthouse impact ≤10, rating threshold, active installs. Re-verify July-2025 category-specific criteria. BFS gives priority placement and plan-based ad targeting.
- **Confirm full visibility** (Partner Dashboard → Distribution → Manage listing) so the listing is indexed in search, category pages, and Google.

## Channel 2 — Content & SEO

- **Comparison / alternative pages**: "[Competitor] alternative", "best back in stock apps for Shopify", "Shopify waitlist app" — bottom-funnel, high-converting.
- **2–4 high-intent blog posts/month**: "recover lost sales from out-of-stock products", "back in stock email/SMS templates that convert", "set up a Shopify restock waitlist". Quality over quantity.
- **Get into third-party roundups** (DelightChat, Meetanshi, Webcontrive). Entry: polished free plan + responsive support, then personalized outreach. Reputable outlets never sell placement.
- **Help docs + YouTube as SEO**: indexable, keyword-targeted docs and a setup walkthrough capture "how-to" traffic and convert hesitant merchants.
- **Mirror keywords into listing meta** (tagline/description = Google title/meta).

## Channel 3 — Paid Ads

- **Shopify App Store ads first.** Search, Category, and Homepage CPC ads (first-price auction). Search ads reward **relevance** — relevant apps pay less and rank higher. Bid on your own category keywords; you compete directly with STOQ/Notify!/Essent. Requires Partner in good standing.
- **Small Google Search budget** on intent terms ("Shopify back in stock app", competitor + "alternative"). Expect ~$3–5 CPC; keep bottom-funnel given $10–50/mo pricing and multi-month CAC payback.
- **Meta for retargeting only** — cold social ads to a niche merchant tool usually lose money.
- **Target ads by merchant plan** (a Built for Shopify perk) to focus spend on plans likeliest to convert.

## Channel 4 — Partnerships & Community

- **Join the Shopify Partner Program** (free; needed for ads anyway).
- **Partner with agencies/Experts** who build stores — become their recommended back-in-stock app.
- **Affiliate/referral program** via UpPromote / ReferralCandy / Refersion. Never spam referral links (account-termination risk).
- **Cross-app co-marketing** (you already integrate Klaviyo): joint content, integration directories, overlapping-audience apps.
- **Community presence**: Shopify Community forums, r/Shopify, r/ecommerce, ecommerce Facebook groups. Be genuinely helpful; don't spam.

---

## Rules to respect

- **No unsolicited emails** to merchants without prior consent (Partner Agreement + CAN-SPAM/CASL).
- **No incentivized or positive-only review asks**; no pressuring merchants over negative reviews.
- **Listing content rules**: no keyword-stuffing/superlatives/guarantees; name ≤30, tagline ≤62, description ≤2,800.
- **No spammy referral-link distribution.**
